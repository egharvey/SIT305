# Additional Apps
These are a series of apps that I made either during workshops or in my own time to further my learning, test something I wasn't sure of or just to have a little bit of fun.

* JavaApp is the app developed in the first weeks workshop (named poorly cause I created it before the workshop and kept accidentally making Kotlin apps)

* PokedexApp is an app that started development in week 3 to get myself familiar with React Native and Expo. It is based off of [this guide by Code with Beto](https://youtu.be/BUXnASp_WyQ?si=ehVuzZ0iwNw0i1GW). **I have currenty yet to expand beyond that**, but going into index.tsx, you can find a to do list I plan to finish off over the course of the trimester

* FragementsTesting was a way to introduce myself to fragments, I followed [this tutorial by CodeLabX](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m-PefJ2CcR8). I do not think it follows good practices, but it served well at introducing me to fragements.

* BottomNavTest was based off of [this guide from Foxandroid](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jOFLmKMOcK0). It goes into a better way of navigating fragments via a bottom nav bar.

* RoomTesting was me loosly following along with [this guide from GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/android/how-to-perform-crud-operations-in-room-database-in-android/)
